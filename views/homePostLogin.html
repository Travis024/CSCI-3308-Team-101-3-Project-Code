Your Recommended Activities



Bear Peak

A must-do hike for anyone in Colorado! The trail is challenging, but the views seen throughout the hike are truly stunning. Plus, a good workout means you can kick back an extra beer after!

Boulder Ale Trail

15 miles, 12 breweries, and 0 cars! The Boulder Ale trail is the perfect way to enjoy your two favorite things in one of the nations biggest brewing cities!

Boulder Creek Path

Go for a stroll or hop on your bike as you travel along the Boulder Creek Path! This path follows the gorgeous Boulder Creek and allows for multiple pitstops at some of Boulders finest locations.

Flatiron Hike

Hike up Boulders most prominent features - the Flatirons! This is a 2.6 mile round trip hike that offers a gorgeous panoramic view at the top.

Flatirons Golf

Enjoy Boulders premier golf course with a wonderful view of the Flatirons! Course regulars love the moderate challenge of the course and the friendly staff.
