About

Since the beginning of mankind, the great outdoors has been a place for humans to find themselves and thrive. However, in today's age, becomming connected with the outdoors is increasingly more difficult. We find ourselves glued to screens, and even when we do have the chance to escape into nature, it's challenging to find a fun activity to get ourselves excited. That's why we created LivBoulder.

What is it?

LivBoulder is a website that allows you to find the perfect outdor activty to fill your day with exciement and joy. Simply click on the 'Register' button to begin setting up your profile, where we'll ask you a few questions about your preferences for activities. After that, we'll match you with hikes, bike rides, adventures, and more that we think are the perfect fit for you. You can also add activities to a list of "Things I Want To Do" and rate activities after you've added them to the list "Things I've Done". No matter what you're looking for, LivBoulder has you covered.

What's the cost?

There is NO cost to LivBoulder. We believe that getting out and enjoying the great outdoors should be a pain-free experience mentally, physically, and on your wallet. So please, enjoy LivBoulder free of charge! All we ask is that you make some incredible memories.


Mission Statement

LivBoulder strives to inspire people to live bolder, more exciting, and more fulfilling lives by connecting them with outdoor experiences right in their own backyard.

How?

At LivBoulder, we're constatnly on the lookout for new activities that we think our members would enjoy. We personally test every activity outselves so that we know exactly who to recommend it to or if we shouldn't even recommend it at all. It might sound like hard work to spend our days out hiking, biking, swimming, and enjoying Boulder, and you're right - it really is. But we care so much about our users that we do it anyways.

Our promise

Our promise to you is that this mission statement will never change. We will always dedicate our time to makign sure that you are able to make the most of of the wonderous things that Mother Nature has to offer. In a world where users can hardly trust any business, we hope to be a beacon of light in a tunnel otherwise filled with darkness.
